Don's Simple Sweet Tea

This Sweet Tea Will Knock Your Socks Off

Sweet Tea: worshipped by some but beloved by all. This simple sweet tea recipe (provided by Don) is sure to make your hot summer days sweet and cool. If you follow these simple instructions, you'll find yourself holding a chill glass of heaven in no time.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups water
  • 4 family-sized tea bags
  • 2 cups white sugar

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot. Dissolve sugar in boiling water; remove from heat
  2. Steep tea bags in water to desired strength of tea, 3 to 5 minutes.
    • Tip: When using individual tea bags, your best option is to remove all the paper endings, and tie the strings together.
  3. Refrigerate tea until cold, at least 1 hour.
